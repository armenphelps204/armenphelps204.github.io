Armenain folk music is the foundation of classical music in Armenia. The greatest Armenian composers studied and took inspiration from the folk tradition. Many wonderful melodies were passed down orally for centuries before later being written down by composers.

Khachatur Avetisyan

  • Avetisyan was a classically trained composer but spent his life researching Armenian folk songs. His compositions often featured centuries-old folk tunes and styles.
  • Below is a sample of Avetisyan’s folk music compositions. His songs are often used as dance music in traditional settings.

Armenian Folk Miniatures, by Komitas

  • In addition to being a classical musician and priest, Komitas traveled the Armenian countryside learning folk songs. He drew from his study of folk music to create light-hearted compositions for choir and solo performance.
