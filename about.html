Who am I?

  • I am currently a third year student at UC Berkeley studying computer science with a passion for programming and theoretical CS.
  • My favorite things to do in my free time are reading fantasy novels, going on hikes, and playing the piano.

Why Armenian music?

  • Growing up as an Armenian-American and attending an Armenian church, I was surrounded by liturgical chants and folk music from Armenian culture. I fell in love with the unique textures and styles in Armenian music.
  • My goal is to continue exploring folk music of Armenia and other ethnicities and share my passion for this music with the world!

What is your favorite piece of Armenian music?

  • I love the “Armenian Miniatures” by Komitas, arranged for string quartet by Sergei Aslamazyan (see the folk music section).
  • There is also a special place in my heart for Komitas’s arrangement of the “Patarag” as I grew up hearing and singing these melodies in my church.

Is this all that Armenian music has to offer? How did you choose what to include?

  • This is definitely not a comprehensive list of Armenian music! There are other wonderful genres like jazz, and modern/pop music and many other composers that have not been mentioned.
  • I chose these genres because they are the most familiar to me from my childhood, but they are only a taste of all the wonderful Armenian music out there!
